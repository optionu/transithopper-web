TransitHopper

TransitHopper is a handy app for the iPhone and Apple Watch that tells you the next departures of public transport services.
Enter your favorite destinations once, then instantly see services departing from your current location.
Currently works in Germany – stay tuned for more countries.
Download on the App Store

Coverage

TransitHopper covers all local and long-distance transport services in Germany – regardless of whether you live in a big city or in the countryside.

Transport Modes

No matter if you travel by subway, train, bus or metro, TransitHopper includes all these transport modes in one app. It also covers long-distance trains.

Realtime Departures

TransitHopper integrates realtime data directly from transit operators. We constantly improve the quality of the data we provide.

See the next departure by swiping up from the watch face.

Get a list of departures by opening the app on the Apple Watch.

Tap on a departure to get more details and find departure stops.
